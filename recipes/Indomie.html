Indomie

Description

This is a noodle styled dish loved by more than half the Population of Nigeria, it is the major go to meal for kids by most Nigerian Parents After a long Day at work

Ingridients

  • Indomie pack, 2 super pack or three Indomitable sizes
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper
  • A white or Purple Onion
  • For Some Greens like Grean Bean, Carrots and Green Bell Pepper
  • Then based on personal preference, we have Suya spice or pepper known as Yaji

Steps

  1. Get your Pot or Pan and put it on your preferred source of heat
  2. for the amount of Noodles specified above, you will need a cup or a cup and half of water, leave it to boil
  3. When thhe water boils you will loosen the noodles from their packs and place them as they are in the pot or pan of boiling water
  4. When the nodles often to a range acceptable to you, you can add your onion, pepper, veggies and spices
  5. allow this to simmer on low heat, turn off your source of heat and serve with a cool chilled drink, Enjoy!
