Joolof Rice

Description

This is a Generally accepted Nigerian Meal, can be found in most restaurants, parties, and gatherings, one of the best meal that the Wonderful West African Country has to offer

  • 4 cups of Rice
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper, chopped
  • A white or Purple Onion, diced or chopped
  • For Some Greens like Grean Bean, Carrots and Green Bell Pepper
  • 8 tomatoes, diced oor chopped
  • salt and maggi for taste

Steps

  1. Get your Pot or Pan and put it on your preferred source of heat
  2. Add your oil to the pan and add your chopped or diced tomatoes ad fry, add your onions and stir, add your pepper and stir
  3. cover pot and allow the ingridients to simmer on low heat
  4. add water or Meat stock if available
  5. add rice and allow to cook on low heat, allow this to simmer on low heat, turn off your source of heat and serve with Yam,Plantain or chips and a cool chilled drink, Enjoy!
